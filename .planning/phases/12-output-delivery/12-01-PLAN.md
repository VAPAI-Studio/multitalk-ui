<objective>
Wire the output delivery pipeline into the video processing flow: after Freepik returns COMPLETED, download the result and upload to Supabase Storage (public URL) and optionally to Google Drive.

Purpose: Videos are currently marked completed with only the Freepik temp URL. This plan makes upscaled videos permanently accessible via Supabase public URLs and optionally backed up to Google Drive, satisfying DLVR-01, DLVR-02, and DLVR-03.

Output: Modified `_process_single_video` with inline delivery, new `upload_upscaled_video` on StorageService, new `update_video_upload_status` on UpscaleJobService, updated UpscaleVideo model with upload status fields, and comprehensive tests.
</objective>
